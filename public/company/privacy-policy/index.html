Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

At PPClicks, we take your privacy seriously. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information. Our goal is simple — be transparent and protect your trust.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to deliver our services and improve your experience:

  • Contact details: name, email, phone, business information
  • Website analytics: IP address, browser type, page interactions
  • Ad performance data: conversions, clicks, impressions, targeting insights
  • Form submissions: audits, consultations, or service inquiries

We never sell your information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide Google Ads management and related services
  • Improve campaign performance and reporting
  • Respond to inquiries and send requested materials (ex: audits)
  • Optimize our website, landing pages, and processes
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

3. Third-Party Tools We Use

To deliver high-quality services, we integrate with trusted third-party platforms:

  • Google Ads & Analytics
  • Google Tag Manager
  • CRM & email automation tools
  • Secure payment processors (if applicable)

These tools may set cookies or track usage to help us improve performance.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Analyze website activity
  • Personalize user experience
  • Improve ad targeting

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Protection & Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. While no system is perfect, we are committed to continually improving security practices.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may request:

  • Access to your stored data
  • Correction or deletion
  • Restriction of processing
  • Opt-out of marketing communication

To exercise your rights, email support@ppclicks.com.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When changes occur, this page will reflect the latest version.

If you have questions, contact us at support@ppclicks.com.
