What are Local Services Ads (LSA)?

Local Services Ads are pay-per-lead ads that appear at the top of Google Search for service-based businesses in your area. They prioritize trust (Google screens providers) and intent — people who use LSAs are often ready to book.

How LSA ties to your business KPIs

  • Bookings / month — we track leads that convert to booked jobs and attribute revenue.
  • Qualified leads — our lead qualification reduces wasted spend on unverified requests.
  • Cost per acquisition (CPA) — optimization targets CPL and downstream CPA (booking cost).
  • Lead-to-booking rate — we report both lead volume and the percentage turning into revenue.
